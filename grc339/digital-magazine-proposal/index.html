Introduction

Offshore

Offshore is a magazine dedicated to capturing the full experience of the surfing lifestyle. It goes beyond basic wave forecasts and competition results to focus on the everyday surfer and the culture that surrounds the sport. Offshore brings together everything that makes surfing more than just a hobby. It highlights the communities, gear, road trips, and the routines that define life on the water. Offshore is a guide to staying connected to the surf world, for both lifelong surfers and beginners.
